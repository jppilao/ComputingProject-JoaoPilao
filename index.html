    About

    In today's globalized world, the intricate relationship between economic prosperity, social dynamics, and environmental sustainability is more evident than ever. This project delves into this complex interplay by analyzing key economic indicators and social metrics across various countries and income levels. Through data analysis and visualization, we aim to uncover insights into how different economic conditions shape societal well-being and environmental impact. By examining trends in GDP, public debt, demographic patterns, and environmental efficiency, we seek to shed light on the challenges and opportunities facing nations in their pursuit of sustainable development.
